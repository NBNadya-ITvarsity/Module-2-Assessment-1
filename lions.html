LIONS

The lion (Panthera leo) is a large cat of the genus Panthera, native to Africa and India.

A group of lions is called a pride which consists of a few male lions, mostly female lions as well their cubs.

Lions are carnivores

The lion inhabits grasslands, savannahs, and shrublands.

You will find the lions at pen A10, by the Northern frontier.
